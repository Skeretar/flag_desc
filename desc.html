THE DESCRIPTION

Peace-Time Flag

The flag is mainly composed of three colours: Red, Blue and White.

The red part represents the blood spilt to get the independence during the war of independence, the blue part represents the Pacific Ocean that is bordering the country, while the dove in it represents the peace and democracy of the country, and white represents the clear skies of the country.

The reason that the flag has this particular shape is a historical reason, which you can check on "HISTORY" section.


War-Time Flag

As you can see, the color blue is completely removed from the flag.

The reason is that the country will enter in a temporary state of emergency while a war, which means that the democratic government will be dissolved until the end of the war. Thus, removing the dove which is supposed to represent peace and democracy.

The meaning of the sun is that it gives hope and strength to the country during the situation.
