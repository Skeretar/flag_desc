THE STORY OF THE FLAG

July 28, 1984

On the 160th century of the Peruvian independence, the new democratic government commisioned a new flag;

Originally, it was supposed to be a simple 2:3 rectangle flag, but at the time there was a shortage of materials, so the flag manufactors decided to cut down some parts.

The dovet and the war-version was introduced later in 1986.
